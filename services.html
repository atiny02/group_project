SERVICE 1

Travel Insurance

(1)Trip Cancellation and Interuption

*Trip Cancellation: Reimburses pre-paid, non-refundable trip expenses if you need to cancel your trip before departure due to covered reasons (e.g., illness, injury, death in the family).

*Trip Interuption: Provides coverage if your trip is cut short due to covered reasons, reimbursing the unused portion of your trip expenses.

(2)Emergency Medical Expenses

Covers medical expenses incurred during the trip due to illness or injury. This can include hospital stays, surgery, prescription medications, and emergency medical evacuation if necessary.

(3)Travel Delay

Reimburses additional expenses incurred due to a covered delay in travel plans, such as accommodation, meals, and transportation.

SERVICE 2

Visa Assistance

(1)Information and Consultation

We provide information about visa requirements for various destinations. This includes details about the type of visa needed, required documents, processing times, and any specific eligibility criteria.

(2)Documentation Assistance

Assisting travelers in preparing and gathering the necessary documents for the visa application. This may include passport photos, proof of accommodation, flight itinerary, travel insurance, financial statements, and invitation letters.

(3)Application Forms and Filling

Helping travelers fill out the visa application forms accurately. These forms can be complex and vary from one country to another, so having assistance can reduce the risk of errors that might delay the application process.

SERVICE 3

24/7 Customer Support

(1)Lost or Delayed Baggage Support

Providing guidance and assistance in case of lost or delayed baggage. This may involve coordinating with airlines and helping travelers track and recover their belongings.

(2)Booking Modifications and Rescheduling

Assistance with modifying or rescheduling travel arrangements due to unexpected changes in plans. This could include rebooking flights, changing accommodation reservations, or adjusting travel itineraries.

(3)Emergency Assistance

Immediate support during emergencies, such as flight cancellations, natural disasters, medical emergencies, or other unforeseen events.
